How to use :

To use this software, just start it, use the slides (1) to adjust the countdown before shutdowning your computer.
Whenever you are ready, press the BIG RED BUTTON (2)! A confirmation pop up will appear, just click ok to continu or cancel to... cancel. If you press ok some Windows consoles will pop up, don't worry, IT'S NORMAL. Just leave your computer, let your thing goes as normal!


If you messed up some thing or simply want to abort :

Don't worry, you've got a smaller, but still, important ABORT ! button (3)on the left of the BIG RED BUTTON!
Simply press it, other Windows consoles will open, still, don't worry, it's nothing, just the cancellation of the shutdown command!
The computer won't stop by itself now!
Again, don't worry, it's not bad for your computer, it will shutdown as usual, just make sure you've saved all you files, you can't blaim me for responsable if you forgot to save your files!
